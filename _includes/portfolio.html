Install Linux

Installing Linux can be a little intimidating. That is where I come in. I make the installation and configuration of Linux easy for you.

I can sit down with you, discuss what you want from Linux and choose the right Linux for you. Taking it one step further I can go over alternative software, configure any software you would like me to and get you off and running with your new Linux install.

Linux Networking

These days, getting Linux working on your current network is fairly simple. You may, however, come across snags when bringing your new Linux install into your existing network.

I can help configure anything from network printing to local run servers. The sky is the limit!

Linux Support

Installing Linux is only the beginning of your adventure, you will come across issues from time to time. I can help you through any issues you may have with your install. Weather you need one-on-one support or wish to get support from our local Linux user group, I am here to help.
